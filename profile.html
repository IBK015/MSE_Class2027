Vision

I believe the best technology feels invisible — not because it hides, but because it becomes so natural it disappears into life itself. My work exists at the intersection of human behaviour and systemic design, creating conditions for people to do the things only they can do.

About

Strategic designer with 12 years shaping digital products across fintech, health, and civic tech. I work at the level of systems — mapping the invisible structures that shape everyday decisions — and translate insight into products that last.

Currently leading design and innovation at Meridian Studio, I partner with leadership teams to define product direction, build design culture, and ship work that endures beyond the next sprint.

Advisor to two Amsterdam-based startups. Occasional speaker on design leadership and regenerative organisations.
